    • Personality

    In the beginning of the series, Musashi was portrayed as a hot headed young man driven by his desire to become strong, with a very aggressive personality. One of the foremost reasons that drives Musashi to excel in sword-fighting, is his desire to overcome his father for all the harsh treatment given during his childhood and Musashi's incapability to rebel. This desire made him violent and feared by others, however Otsu was the only person who didn't fear him. In Vagabond he was obsessed with being "Invincible under the Heavens" until he realises that it was simply an empty title and how limited he was. When he was asked the definition of "Invincible under the Heavens", he compared it to a heat haze where it gets hot when one strives towards it but it fades when one reaches it. After destroying the Yoshioka clan, he becomes more gentle and peaceful but he understands he can't escape the spiral of death and killing. As he figured out that he's stuck in the spiral of death and killing, he felt killing others was like stabbing himself.

    Relationships

    Musashi's relationships with those around him shape his journey and character. His childhood friend Otsu becomes a significant figure in his life, as they bond over their shared loneliness. Musashi realizes he loves her but distances himself to protect her from the dangers of his life, though he later expresses a desire to live with her. Another childhood friend, Matahachi Honiden, was once considered Musashi's best friend, but their paths diverge after Matahachi’s betrayal. When they reunite, Musashi is deeply saddened by how much Matahachi has changed. Soho Takuan, an eccentric monk, serves as a mentor to Musashi, using harsh but effective methods to teach him valuable lessons, helping him grow both as a warrior and as a person. Sasaki Kojiro, initially not an enemy, shares an unspoken connection with Musashi, and they are instinctively drawn to each other when they meet again. Jotaro, Musashi's first disciple, forms a close bond with him as they both strive to become stronger. Although Musashi tries to leave Jotaro for his betterment, Jotaro and Otsu continue searching for Musashi, while Musashi occasionally wonders about Jotaro’s well-being during his journey.

    Abilities

    Musashi is unique in terms of his sword-skill for the unfamiliar movements and lack of style he uses. This is due to the fact that he spent most of his entire time in the forest having been labelled as a demon child by his village for his demeaning and wicked nature. Frankly, the forest is his home and where his master resides. He is known for his arm strength, in the Yagyū arc he was able to cut a steel sword with one hand. Swordsmanship: Niten Ichi-ryū (二天一流; literally meaning: "Two Heavens as One Style") - he created the two sword style by wielding a katana on the offensive right hand while the Wakizashi on the defensive left hand. He questioned himself why Japanese swordsman only use one sword during fights. He displayed full use of the two sword against Tsujikaze Kōhei/Shishido Baiken. One of the secrets of the technique is to throw the wakizashi blade like a shuriken/kunai.
